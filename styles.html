Yoga Styles

Yoga trends seem to be ‘the thing’ – so I am currently working on developing the following styles, message me if you’re interested in any:

  • Joe-Ga: An invigorating, fun and heartwarming class for all people named Joe
  • Laderhoga: German beer, hearty meal, and yoga in excellent multipurpose trousers
  • So?-ga: Reconnect with the core rather than the ego. You can hold a headstand – So? But how does the headstand actually make you feel?
  • To-ga: If you don’t know what to wear to this class, you are not enlightened enough to attend.
  • Lo-ga: Think you can limbo?
  • YO-GAaaaarrr: Y’Think ya can hold a plank? But can ya walk it?
  • Ogre: A strengthening practice to release the inner beast in you
  • Crow-Ga: Release the crows! (Crows will not be provided. Please bring your own Crow.)
  • YO-GAndalf: Master class in balance poses. Fly, you fools
  • Pogo-ga: A challenging flow performed entirely on one leg. Two class course recommended to achieve full equilibrium on both legs
