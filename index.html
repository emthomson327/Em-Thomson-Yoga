Hello! Welcome to my Yoga website

I’m not a huge fan of the whole spiel about what yoga means to me personally – breath, mind, body are key – but beyond those it means different things to everyone – and that is why I love it.

I believe yoga is accessible to all and it is a practical way to see how we each develop in our own special ways. Some people will be awesome at backbends, others rock at arm balances whilst some are wizards of the mind and meditation. Everyone’s their own special kind of superhero, you just need to take the time to see it – which kind are you?

  • I trained at Sampoorna Yoga, in Goa, India
  • Fully accredited and insured by Yoga Alliance UK.
  • Available to teach Vinyasa Flow, Ashtanga, Hatha, Core Power flow.
  • Also available for yoga for actors, one-to-one private sessions, corporate yoga, lunch-time office yoga, and private party hire.
